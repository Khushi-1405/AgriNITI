Executive Summary

This AI/ML-driven platform provides real-time insights into agricultural markets, supply chains, and price dynamics for better decision-making and policy interventions by ministries to ensure food security and market stability.

Problem Statement

Traditional methods lack the speed and detail required for real-time agricultural monitoring, leading to missed opportunities and market instability that affects both farmers and consumers.

Project Goals & Objectives

  • Scrape and unify real-time agri-market data from APIs.
  • AI/ML-driven price predictions and risk alerts.
  • Policy suggestions using anomaly and sentiment analysis.
  • Interactive dashboards and real-time map-based risk insights.

Proposed Solution & Methodology

The system integrates web scraping, time series forecasting, anomaly detection, yield estimation, and hunger risk mapping using satellite and socio-economic data. AI models generate insights, while the dashboard displays alerts and maps for policy action.

Project Overview

This platform aggregates real-time agricultural market data and visualizes food security risks for proactive policymaking.
